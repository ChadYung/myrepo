Diet Advice

Find out your maintenance calories on this Site

CALORIE SURPLUS: To gradually GAIN weight, eat at about 300-400 calories MORE than your maintenance calories.

CALORIE DEFICIT: To gradually LOSE weight, eat at about 400-500 calories LESS than your maintenance calories.

Protien

Protien should account for 30% of your Calorie intake

Recomended Meat Protien Sources: Chicken, Beef, Turkey, Tuna

Recomended Vegetarian Protien Sources: Beans, Eggs, Greek Yogurt, Cheese

Supplements: Protien Powder

Carbs

Carbs should account for 50% of your Calorie intake

Recomended Carb Sources: Rice, Bread, Fruits, Milk

Fats

Fats should account for 20% of your Calorie intake

Recomended Fat Sources: Avocados, Salmon, Nuts, Eggs
